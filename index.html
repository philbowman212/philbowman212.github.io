About

My name is Phil and I'm a data professional with 8+ years of Python experience and 7+ years working with SQL. The sea of data is vast and growing every day - I enjoy diving in to discover insights and build solutions that make a real impact.

Currently, I'm a Workforce Analytics Developer at OSF HealthCare, where I build dashboards, develop data pipelines, and translate business needs into technical solutions. My toolkit includes Python, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, PySpark, Oracle, Azure DevOps, Power Automate, and various AI and CLI tools. I also work extensively with workforce systems like UKG and iCIMS. Previously, I was a Business Intelligence Analyst at Gate City Bank, partnering with executives to develop reporting solutions and KPIs.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Actuarial Science from Bradley University, giving me a solid foundation in mathematics and statistics. I've explored NLP, sports analytics, and recommendation systems through personal projects. Before charting my course into data, I spent several years in retail management at Walgreens, where I developed skills in team leadership and operational metrics.
